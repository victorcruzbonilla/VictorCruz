Profile

About me

I am a Development Economist with deep expertise in macroeconomic policy analysis, international trade, and macro-financial risk assessment. My work bridges rigorous economic analysis and practical policy design across government, financial institutions, academia, and multilateral organizations.

Recognized as Economista Distinguido del Año 2025 by COLPROCE, I have represented El Salvador in international trade negotiations and developed data-driven tools adopted at the ministerial level. As a researcher-educator, I teach at the graduate and undergraduate levels at three Salvadoran universities.

My research interests include development finance, trade policy, macro-financial vulnerabilities in emerging economies, and the role of data analytics in public policy design.
